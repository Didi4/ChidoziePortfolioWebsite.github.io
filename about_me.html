About Me

Hi, my name is Chidozie and I am a data analyst.
I hold a Bachelor’s Honours degree in Computer Science(University of Birmingham, England)
and Master’s degree in Data Analytics (Aston University, England).

I'm a driven data analyst whose background in Computer Science and Data Analytics, honed my analytics skills. I have hands-on experience leveraging tools like Python, SQL, Tableau, and Excel to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets.

My interest in data science began during undergrad while taking advanced courses in machine learning, data mining, and statistics. I was fascinated by the patterns and trends that could be uncovered using analytical techniques. This motivated me to continue building expertise in data analytics methodologies and technologies through postgraduate education and self-guided learning.

Today I have 2+ years of experience gathering, understanding, modeling, and interpreting large datasets to create actionable solutions for diverse audiences. My skills include data processing, exploratory analysis, predictive modeling, classification algorithms, A/B testing, and translating technical findings into compelling narratives and visualizations. I stay up-to-date on the latest tools and techniques.

While I enjoy the technical work, my true passion is using data to tell impactful stories that inform strategic decision making. I've experience delivering presentations explaining analyses to teams. Ensuring all stakeholders can act on insights is my top priority.

When I'm not crunching numbers, I enjoy playing and watching football, leadership activities, and reading about the latest technologies. Please take a look at my portfolio projects on the other page to see examples of my analytical work! I'm excited to utilize my multifaceted data skills to drive tangible impacts.
